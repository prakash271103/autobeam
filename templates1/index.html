Design and Detailing of RCC Beams















Our Beam Automation Project is a sophisticated tool designed to streamline the creation of detailed DXF drawings for various types of beams, including simply supported and cantilever beams. This application automates complex calculations and drawing processes, ensuring precision and efficiency.

Based On :IS 456,SP 16,SP 24,IS 13920

Key Features:

  • Automated Calculations: Performs intricate calculations for shear forces, bending moments, and more.
  • Customizable Drawings: Generates DXF files with customizable dimensions and annotations, including section details and reinforcement layouts.
  • Interactive Interface: Easy-to-use web interface for inputting beam parameters and downloading the generated drawings.
  • High Precision: Ensures accurate placement of elements like text, lines, and circles within the drawings.
  • Scalable Solution: Capable of handling various beam types and configurations, making it a versatile tool for civil engineers and designers.

Explore our Beam Automation Project to experience a seamless and efficient way of creating professional-grade beam drawings.
